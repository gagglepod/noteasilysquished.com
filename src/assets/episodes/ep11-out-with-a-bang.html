Today, here in the Valley of the Dinos it's the Election Eve before the most important election in our lifetime -- or so they keep saying. This is when we, as Americans, leave milk and cookies out for Captain America before getting a rough and restless night of insomnia followed by going to the polls tomorrow. I'm not going to tell you who to vote for because you already know who you need to vote for. Instead, I've created an Election Mix that you can use while you wait your turn in line. I made it special with limited interruptions so that you can get a 30-minute power session in right before you go into the booth or drown out the smelly hippy standing next to you in line. Heck, you could even ride your mountain bike down to the city center and make a day of it. But here's to you, fellow American! Mr. Murphy and I salute you. Now, go make Uncle Sam proud!


About Flowgnar

Flowgnar, hosted by Kyle M. Bondo, is a music podcast for outdoor enthusiasts who need a soundtrack for their next adventure. It features a curated playlist of Valueverse artists that turn garage band-like beats into pure endurance fuel. Learn more at flowgnar.com.


Trail Tested Playlist

I have personally trail-tested each of my playlists on my mountain bike -- a 2019 Kona HeiHei CR/DL called Chickensaurus-Rex -- to flow you through 10 miles of Texas heat, rock, and gnar. Then again on foot with Salomon Speedcross 6 trail shoes to make sure it can hold up to a good 5-mile trail running. Then I got in a good 3-mile ruck hike with 30 lbs of added weight to triple down on a playlist that has been certified to keep you moving. This is the Flowgnar Guarantee: If you embrace the flow, I will give you tunes to help you chew the gnar! Only on Flowgnar!
