Welcome to Flowgnar Episode #4 -- Fossil Fuel Injector! I am your Summer Time trail guide Kyle M. Bondo, coming to you from The Valley of the Dinosaurs -- Glen Rose, Texas. Today marks the last month of Summer and I just celebrated a birthday in July. This makes August the grand finale to my Month-Long Birthday Celebration! I love a good July and August, especially when you get to live to see the next one. That is my Parking Lot wisdom for you today. About Flowgnar

Flowgnar, hosted by Kyle M. Bondo, is a music podcast for outdoor enthusiasts who need a soundtrack for their next adventure. It features a curated playlist of Valueverse artists that turn garage band-like beats into pure endurance fuel.

Trail Tested Playlist

Each playlist has been personally trail tested by Kyle on his mountain bike -- a 2019 Kona HeiHei CR/DL called Chickensaurus-Rex -- to flow you through 10 miles of Texas heat, rock, and gnar. It should be good enough for 5 miles of trail running or a good 3-mile hike too. Embrace the flow while you chew the gnar! Learn more at flowgnar.com.
