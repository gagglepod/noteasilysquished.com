Today is one of those days where I didn't want to go outside. I wanted to stay inside, eat junk food, and watch YouTube all day. But if I skip Monday's ride, I'll most likely skip Tuesday's ride too. Then, before you know it, I've skipped half the week and now it's Thursday! Geesh! This is the theme of today's mix: doing it even when you don't want to. I get that you need to listen to your body and rest when it needs it. However, I've found that my body -- and those dark voices in my head -- can trick you into believing you need rest when what you really need is to hit the trail and knock the dirt off your dinosaur bones! So, that's what I'm going to do today. I'm going to go ride even though I don't want to. Are you with me? Then let's get this Paleo Powerhouse moving. Go, go, GO!
