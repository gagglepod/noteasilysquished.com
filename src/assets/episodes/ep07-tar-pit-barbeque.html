Welcome to Flowgnar Episode #6 -- Mesozoic Life Crisis! I am your Summer Time trail guide Kyle M. Bondo, coming to you from The Valley of the Dinosaurs -- Glen Rose, Texas. Today marks the last week of Summer! Oh, no! August here in Texas has been hot, hot, hot! But strangely not as hot as last year. To quote The Hives: A time was invented to keep the kids in the hall, they gave it a name and we all know it is 'Fall'. But that's just the line of the story that I have presented: Fall is just something that grown-ups invented! That is my Parking Lot wisdom for you today. Just because it's Fall doesn't mean it is the end of your adventures. So take this curated playlist of Valueverse artists and tortured dinosaur metaphors and get moving. Go, go, GO!


About Flowgnar

Flowgnar, hosted by Kyle M. Bondo, is a music podcast for outdoor enthusiasts who need a soundtrack for their next adventure. It features a curated playlist of Valueverse artists that turn garage band-like beats into pure endurance fuel.


Trail Tested Playlist

Each playlist has been personally trail tested by Kyle on his mountain bike -- a 2019 Kona HeiHei CR/DL called Chickensaurus-Rex -- to flow you through 10 miles of Texas heat, rock, and gnar. It should be good enough for 5 miles of trail running or a good 3-mile hike too. Embrace the flow while you chew the gnar! Learn more at flowgnar.com.
