Today, I'm reminded of what Marcus Aurelius said about dwelling on problems: "Your days are numbered, and if you don't clear the clouds from your mind now you may never have another chance." What does that mean? You cannot dwell on failures and be dissatisfied with today because you may not have a tomorrow. You have to live your life as if every moment were your last. In other words, be thankful for today because tomorrow is not guaranteed. So if you woke up on this side of the dirt today I want you to put on your Big Toothy Grin and remind yourself to let that stuff go and get back to living in the moment. Are you with me? Then let's let that stuff go and get to it.
