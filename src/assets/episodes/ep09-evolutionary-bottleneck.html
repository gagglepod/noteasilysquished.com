Today, I had to put myself in someone else's shoes after getting into a big argument with a best friend and realizing that I was the one in the wrong. What do about it? You do the only thing you can do, you ask for forgiveness! That is the lesson for today -- if something is bothering you don't keep it inside. Talk about it now! Second, if you do get into a fight and say something dumb it's not the end of the world. Once the hurt feelings calm down you can work it out but only if you realize that the only person you can control is YOU! Finally, and most importantly, when you do mess up (and you will), try saying you're sorry. It goes a long way.
